## 4. Data Security

### Our Security Measures
We implement industry-standard protections:

🔒 **Encryption**
- All data transmitted via HTTPS (SSL/TLS)
- Sensitive data encrypted at rest
- Authentication tokens encrypted

🔒 **Authentication**
- Google OAuth authentication (no passwords stored)
- Session-based security
- Automatic session timeout

🔒 **Access Control**
- Limited access to personal data
- Employees sign confidentiality agreements
- Regular security audits

🔒 **Compliance**
- GDPR compliant
- CCPA compliant
- PCI-DSS standards for payment data

### What We DON'T Store
- ❌ Credit card full numbers (Stripe handles this)
- ❌ CVV codes
- ❌ Plain text passwords

## 5. Cookies

### What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them for:

**Essential Cookies** (required for site functionality)
- Session authentication
- CSRF protection
- User preferences

**Analytics Cookies** (optional, help us improve)
- Traffic analysis
- User behavior tracking
- Performance monitoring

### Cookie Consent
- We request consent on first visit
- You can disable cookies in browser settings
- Some features may not work without essential cookies

### Cookie Settings
In your browser:
1. Settings → Privacy/Security
2. Manage cookies
3. Clear cookies anytime
